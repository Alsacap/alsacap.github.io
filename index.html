A Capital Growth Focused Firm

Our mission is to look for the opportunities around the United States and take position in them before they become popular in the market. From public to private companies, currencies to commodities, and startups to large business, ALSACAP will be there before the crowd. This goal will be achieved by investing our resources in innovative research to find in advance which direction the market and people's sentiment move to next. Our research resources range from global indices analysis to personal connections with market makers.

We use an aggressive investment approach, yet we are able to reduce the down risk significantly by diversifying our portfolio between different types of securities and significantly hedging the high risk - high reward securities. In addition, we place our capital in the highest yield forms of securities and constantly monitor factors such as volatility and economical data to alert us in case of an incoming shift in the market direction.

The market will always reward those who understand it’s current and future trends, rather than the ones who try to outsmart it and move against its direction. As an early stage firm, we have to think in a modern way of how today’s investing world moves, rather than traditional thinking. The increase in retail investor market transactions caused an increase in volume and volatility to the markets. Our job is to insure we benefit from the market volatility, and rapidly adapt to how investors think and behave. To maximize our returns, we move our money with the market’s sentiment and we do not reject an opportunity just because the traditional way of thinking doesn’t support it.

The charts below compares the YoY performance of the Top funds managed in the United states, Dow Jones Industrial Average, and ALSACAP. The aggressive market strategy ALSACAP executed resulted in the firm beating the Dow by 123 points, and the top U.S funds by 115 points. In other words, if you invested just $10,000 with us, the firm would have returned you $23,117 at the year-end compared to just $10,725 from the Dow, or $11,526 from the top U.S funds. Our investing strategy proves that chasing high returns by investing in riskier assets while aggressively hedging against the downfall generates unique returns to our investors while maximizing the portfolio protection.

Investment Categories in Focus

  • Public Companies

    Picking quality stocks that will generate high amount of returns to the firm as value and growth are in focus. Our plan targets a diversified portfolio in Technology, Consumer Cyclical, Green Energy, and Fintech companies which will generate the highest returns over the upcoming years.

    Small Business Lending

    Providing short term lending to small businesses at the lowest interest rate, or in exchange for shares in the business. This helps the small business achieve their short-term goals while not having to adjust their budget due to high interest rates and allow better usage of the funds awarded.

  • Indices

    Because they provide an overall exposure to specific markets, Indices are a great fit for investment growth when betting into economies. From commodities like gold and oil to currencies like the dollar, Indices provide the exposure needed to the firm's portfolio to these economies.

    Commercial Real Estate

    We take minority stakes in a variety of commercial real estate projects under development around the United States. This strategy allows limited exposure to the overall economy conditions while benefitting from the future capital growth that derives from the gain in the estate value.

  • Corporate Bonds

    Corporate Bonds provide a double benefit to our portfolio, the annual interest collected from the issuer, while still being able to sell it in secondary markets. It's also considered one of the safe havens form of securities as long as the business stay healthy and maintain a low risk level.

    Residential Real Estate

    We take stakes in healthy residential properties directly from the owner in the most attractive homes around America. Homeowners will benefit from the funds while they remain the majority owners of their home. As the housing market value grows over time, our stakes in the housing market grow with it as well.

  • StartUps

    Investors worldwide pour hundreds of millions of dollars annually to Silicon Valley attempting to find the next big unicorn. It takes an in-depth research and analysis of a start up's delivered product and the future consumer preference to pick the right one to take a position in.

  • CDs

    Investing in CDs delivers assurance to the portfolio as a short-term low risk investment due to the investment accounts being Federally Insured. CDs have very low fees which allows more predictable returns. CDs and Corporate Bonds are our two low risk investments

  • Events

    Investing in seasonal events is a profitable move yet less common duo to it being limited to private investors mostly. Being connected to the biggest seasonal events organizers is our goal to be part of the functions and generate sizable return to our investment within this category.
